Let's assume you want to run a 5K marathon, you can use the principles of the 5Ps to develop habits that will help you achieve your goal and prepare you for that long run. The 5Ps can guide you to create a successful plan that will fit into your daily routine. As you can see the 5Ps principles can be applied to build habits, individual projects or client projects.

Presentation: Think about your environment, do you have a space at home or a gym membership to run? If not, do research in you area about where you can go running.

Practice: Plan where, at what time and for how long you will run. After you research where you will go running, practice going for a short amount of time to build the habit of running.

Production: Mark it in your calander, when, at what time, and for how long you will run. Keep doing this and add more distance until you get to 5k.

Posting: Keep your self accountable, tell people! Your family and friends will help you with accountability; motivation does not last forever.

Participation: After you have build the habit of running long distances, you can sign up to run a 5K marathon and celebrate your accomplisment with other particpants.
